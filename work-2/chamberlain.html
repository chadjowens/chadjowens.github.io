Chaimberlain College of Nursing

Site Redesign and User Testing

Overview

Chamberlain College of Nursing engaged Rockfish Interactive (now part of VMLY&R) to completely revamp their website. This included a massive content audit and content strategy effort prior to beginning with the screen designs for desktop and mobile.

As part of this project, I also created fully clickable wireframes that the team took into extensive user testing sessions. The project was considered a great success with the client and set the stage for ongoing work.

Site Design Screen Samples
  • The site redesign focused on large emotive visuals to convey the idea that nurses often describe their job as very "fulfilling" as their purpose is to help others.

    The site also utilized cards / tiles to provide actionable pathways to higher traffic content beyond the main navigation.

Interaction Design and Experience Architecture
  • The Interaction Design / Experience Architecture took quite a bit of content wrangling for this assignment.

    Prior to defining the overall site structure I had to cull through a lot of content from various sites and print materials to determine what could be leveraged and repurposed.

Mobile Design Screen Samples
  • This work was near the time responsive site design was becoming popular but not quite mainstream.

    Chamberlain chose to move forward with an adaptive mobile solution.

    Since the purpose of the site was mostly lead generation and educational, the mobile site was organized in simple to navigate accordion tabs.
