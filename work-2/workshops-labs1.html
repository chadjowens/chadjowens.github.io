Northern Trust

Product Ideation Workshops 
During my time at UXFactory, we began to form a relationship with the Northern Trust product delivery teams responsible for tablet and handset applications. To help drive the relationship forward, we tailored a series of educational and workshop presentations that covered a wide variety of topics related to digital product design.

These efforts culminated in an assignment to tailor content for an ideation workshop specific to a particular feature Northern Trust was interested in adding to their product roadmap. 

Workshop slide excerpts.
The workshop included the following process: 
  • Reviewing key concepts from education series presentations
  • Team break out into three groups - each representing multi-disciplined roles
  • Ideate and work through a Customer Needs Analysis framework to align key features proposed to real audience persona needs
  • Ideate and work through a "Use Context Framework" to help illustrate multiple ways the product feature can be designed based on a variety of contexts
  • 3 concepts were created for further refinement and concept testing
