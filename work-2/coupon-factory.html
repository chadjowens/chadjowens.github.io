Coupon  Factory

Incubator & Product Development Team

The Assignment

Rockfish Interactive had a unique perspective on digital product development and innovation being core core to their agency operations. Part of this focus resulted in several stand alone new businesses being spun out of Rockfish.

Coupon Factory was one of these efforts that I helped bring to life.

https://adage.com/article/digital/rockfish-tool-churns-digital-coupons-brands/226911

Business Benefits

  1. Provide a turn key solutions that allowed brands to:
    - Create and manage customized coupons,
    - Deliver them vis mobile of desktop web experiences,
    - Manage of affiliate marketers and locations , and
    - Track the performance of coupon campaigns.
  2. Drive brand awareness through digital couponing placements on social channels

Process & Activities Overview 

  1. Rapid Design working sessions with product owner and product engineers to define product purpose and how it should work.
  2. Design sprints to define solution and resolve questions prior to moving feature into development.
  3. Rapid internal usability feedback loops incorporated into agile design sessions.
  4. Provided static wireframe and design comps as well as clickable prototype to development team.
  5. Supported feature enhancements as product evolved as well as designed mobile app version of the experience.

Coupon Factory Desktop Experience
  • Product landing page provides a tabbed approach that starts with an overlay that gives the user an overview of how the product works.
  • A dashboard provides an analytics view of coupon progress from the following parameters:
    - Impressions,
    - Clicks,
    - Prints,
    - Clickthrough Rate, and
    - Conversion
  • A step-by-step coupon creator flows allows a user to quickly create and publish coupons and set up locations where coupons can be redeemed.
  • Once created the user can select which social channels (or affiliate sites) they would like to publish to.

Coupon Factory Mobile Experience

  • A mobile app experience was also created to provide a simple streamlined way to engage with the platform while on the go.
