Northern Trust - Education Series

Presentation and Mini Workshop – “Product Mindfulness"
During my time at UXFactory, we began to from a relationship with Northern Trust product delivery teams responsible for tablet an handset product delivery. To help drive the relationship forward we tailored a series of educational and workshop presentations that covered a wide variety of topics within the User Experience field and specifically related to aspects of digital product design.

This educational series became a platform of interest within a number of organization’s mobile and innovation teams who asked to setup the presentation series for their teams. The education series / mini workshop was separated into three components:

Product Mindfulness, Design Mindfulness and Process Mindfulness

Presentation / Workshop Slide Excerpts

As part of the Product Mindfulness presentation and mini workshop we touched on concepts such as:
  1. The difference (and value) of uniform design vs. unified design in omni-channel experience planning and solution design.
  2. Ideation frameworks such as Needs/Values, Use Context and Solutions Curve that help drive team discussion around product features, long term road map planning and long-term value propositions
  3. Team decision frameworks that allow Agile team structures to be more efficient decision makers based on pre-determiend guiding principles.
