Sam's Club Everyday Meals

Site Architecture and Design

Overview

While working at Rockfish Interactive (now part of VMLY&R), I worked on a number of site experiences for various campaigns. One simple site that I worked on that happens to be a favorite was an experience called Every Day Meals.

This was a simple concept executed very well that showcased and featured various products and provided a range of recipes for each product in effort to promote product awareness and sales. Content strategy and site architecture were key components to this process as there were many ways existing content could be leveraged and repurposed.

Site Design Screen Samples

The site design leveraged the brand color palette that we worked with for many of the Sam's brand sites we worked on.

However, this site experience functioned more as a small catalog intended to showcase and highlight the food and recipes. Therefore, the design aesthetic utilized more ope white space.

Interaction Design and Experience Architecture Samples

Interaction design and site architecture came after stakeholder working sessions and extensive content review and audits. Interaction designs included detailed annotations / specifications.
