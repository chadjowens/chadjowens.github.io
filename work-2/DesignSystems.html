Design Systems and Component Libraries

Overview

A typical part of our process at Drawn was to develop Design Systems when needed for certain engagements .

These artifacts were considered "never final" and the team constantly added and updated based on new design solutions that were needed. Design systems helped the design process to be more efficient and consistent. They also provided the developer certain specifications and guidelines.

Not shown here, but the design systems also lived in an interactive component library tool that allowed the development team to view, comment and pull down certain front end development styling attributes .
