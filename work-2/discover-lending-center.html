Lending Center Platform

The Assignment

The purpose of the Lending Platform assignment was to integrate Discover’s credit management product offering at the user interface level, within the DFS Account Center. Both Balance Transfers and Discover Personal Loans would be offered within the platform, along with tools and educational content.  

This was intended to provide customers with a single place to manage credit and educate them on their options. The integrated options would make borrowing more convenient and accessible to customers. It also enabled consumers to make more educated lending choices based on their personal circumstances.

Business Benefits

  1. Expand credit options and provide tools to help customers pay down their debt better.
  2. Integrate Balance Transfers (BTs) and Discover Personal Loans (DPLs) into a single “Lending Platform” in the Discover Card Account Center that will provide a clearer and simpler way to shop for solutions. 
  3. Provide payment flexibility by allowing the consumer to choose how much to pay.

Process & Activities Overview 

  1. Rapid Design Lab working sessions to uncover design innovation opportunities and validate implementation complexities
  2. Detailed functional requirements gathering and project definition
  3. Full interaction design concepts and Axure prototype for concept testing and design research
  4. Complete interaction design documentation and interaction specifications
  5. Visual design
  6. Iterative design recommendations based on User Research findings
