Nutro

In-Store Sales Support iPad Application

The Assignment

The Nutro Pet Food company was receiving increasing competitive threat around their in-store Pet Nutrition Specialist (PNS) concept. This was a high-touch program intended to help educate consumers on key product differences and drive brand awareness during retail store visits.

To bolster their competitive advantage in this area, Nutro was looking for a partner to assist in the concepting, design and development of a new tool. The tool would assist the PNS team with guiding consumers toward the best Nutro pet food solution for their pet. It also provided the nutrition specialists with a comprehensive source of information to support tailored content-driven conversations with consumers.

Business Benefits

Tailoring the food & treat selections to a very specific breed and age of the shopper's pet provided the following benefits:
  1. Saves the customer time in store trying to compare brands
  2. Eases their concerns or doubts about the quality of the ingredients
  3. Bolsters their trust that Nutro is a brand that cares about quality
  4. Provides a fun and engaging experience that associates the brand with usefulness and innovation.
