My Sustainability Plan

The Assignment

Walmart asked for a flexible application (My Sustainability Plan) as part of enhancement to to it's internal employee portal as a way to nudge employees to create and track meaningful personal goals.

The application would allow store associates to create and track goals that reflect personal lifestyle improvements they desired to make. Therefore, the application was geared heavily on the concepts of persuasion design. It utilized achievements, variable rewards and even competition to drive engagement.

This tool became a core component of the main myWalmart.com intranet. The program was rolled out nationally as a beta test prior to developing multi-language extensions and launching globally.  

Product screens with visual design applied.

The visual design applied provided a light and airy feel to an experience that had a lot of backend functionality. User's were able to easily and intuitively understand how to navigate the application experience and take advantage of the features and functionality offered.
‍

Wireframes with annotations.

The wireframe concepts were created with heavily documented interaction notes. This allowed the team to communicate complicated business logic and screen states to the client.

The application allows a user to create multiple goals, track goals with daily check-ins, gain achievements and unlock reward content.  Driving engagement through competition; by allowing associates to form teams and challenge other store locations, was another key feature.
