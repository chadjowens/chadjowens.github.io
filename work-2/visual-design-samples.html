Overview

In addition to the other larger project work samples I've shown, below are a few additional samples of visual designs that I've either directed or produced.

Cisco (Corporate Social Responsibility Site) 
  • While at Rockfish, I had an opportunity to work on Corporate Social Responsibility site for Cisco.
  • This screen became be part of a number of concepts presented to the stakeholders.

Sam's Club (Mom's Community)
  • This is a design example pulled from concepts that were created for a Sam's Club and P&G partnership site called Mom's Community.

Fidipidi (Card Creation Web Application)
  • While at Acquity Group we worked on an extensive business design program to help an old school card printing company rethink their business in a more digital first way.
  • This screen represents a card creation web application that was created as a new brand extension that would allow consumers to create very detailed custom cards. Cards would be printed on site and shipped to consumers.

MGPI (Content Strategy and Site Redesign)
  • This is a home page design example pulled from a large content strategy and full site redesign for project I worked on for our client MGP.

Shift 365
  • Concepts for small marketing and communications firm site redesign.
  • This site redesign incorporated motion design and dramatically simplified the site content and offered very clear navigational "actionable pathways."

Chamberlain College of Nursing
  • The site redesign focused on large emotive visuals to convey the idea that nurses often describe their job as very "fulfilling" as their purpose is to help others.

    The site also utilized cards / tiles to provide actionable pathways to higher traffic content beyond the main navigation.
