The Assignment

Staples engaged T3 to help them rethink an iconic brand signature moment. The assignment was to rethink how the easy button could be turned into a more useful experience. The solution was to design and develop a "Staples Easy System." This system included a revamped hardware IOT Easy Button device as well as a Chatbot feature that could be initiated from the Staples native mobile app. This system, powered by Watson, would allow inputs as voice, text and images.

Business Benefits

  1. Allows users to engage with Staples through a variety of methods that remove friction and is easy as talking or typing.
  2. Removes the burden of customer service associates by automating and servicing more routine questions or orders.
  3. Positions Staples as an innovator in its class relative to peers and competition.

Process & Activities Overview 

  1. Define agile sprint cadence and deliverable milestones.
  2. Define conversation parameters (Entities, Intents, etc) and design the dialog flow.
  3. Develop UI and screen flows with all paths and fallbacks.
  4. Build prototype "vignette" flows for each use case with InVision (as well as experiments with live data using ChatFuel).
  5. Stakeholder review and iterate based on feedback
