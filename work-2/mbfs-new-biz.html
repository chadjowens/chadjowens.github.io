Journeys, Key Moments & Design Concepts

The Assignment

During my time a T3, I led the experience design concept development for Mercedes Benz Financial Services. This effort included developing a Trends Analysis, Heuristic Evaluation, Personas and Journey Maps prior to developing any of the interaction design or creative artifacts. This effort also included a newly defined agile agency process and approach that I developed in coordination with our organization's CTO and COO.

Each of the below design solutions tie specifically to key moments that were outlined in the Persona and Journey Maps (shown lower on the screen).

Visual Design Concepts

Visual designs were created based off of several persona key moments defined. The screen designs were were created with a responsive layout in mind and laid out in a way that could easily degrade into smaller viewports for tablet and mobile devices.
‍
The process began with developing content around trends, a Heuristic Analysis and how the design solution would pay off the brand promise that our brand strategists had defined as part of the approach. Additionally, I defined how design systems elements would work across multiple device types and other branded products that use the same financial services portal (such as SMART cars).
‍
High level Personas and User Journeys were created to guide the design efforts. Designs were based off of "Key Moments" that were laid out within the User Journeys.
‍
The solution design effort began with working sessions to define high-level concepts. Those were then quickly refined through a wireframing process. Finally, visual designs were applied to bring the solutions to life.
