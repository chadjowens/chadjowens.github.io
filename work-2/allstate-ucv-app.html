Allstate

Innovation Team - Agent iPad Application

The Assignment

Allstate had several internal projects in flight related to allowing agents to service their customers through improved access to consumer data. This was part of an ongoing effort to strengthen the brand positioning of around Allstate agents being considered "Trusted Advisors" in the customer's mind.
‍

Tablet screen design samples.  

Moving quickly from whiteboard sketch sessions, I mocked up several visual design concepts that would determine the overall flow of the experience.

I also mocked up several interactive experiences leveraging Proto.io that allowed stakeholders to see and experience how the micro-interactions, animations, gesture controls could work across the various concepts.
‍

Information architecture & screen flows.

Once a concept direction was selected, I established the overall screen flow as well as the agile design cadence for screens to be reviewed and then batched to the native app dev team.
‍

Wireframes with annotations.

In addition to a functional prototype created in Proto.io, each deliverable batch included a wireframe technology hand-off package. This deliverable included detailed functional specifications and page state annotations that could not be documented in the prototype.
