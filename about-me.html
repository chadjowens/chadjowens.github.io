About Me

My Process
I’m a dad, a husband, an DIYer, a maker and craftsman at heart.

I transitioned from the traditional architectural design field well over ten years ago and have had the good fortune of shaping digital products, commerce and marketing touch points for some of the biggest companies in existence.

I spend unhealthy amounts of time thinking about users, behavior, psychology, interactions and design thinking as they are all components that feed into delivering world class user-centered products and brand engagement solutions.

I've spent the past 12 years being on, working with and building teams of Digital Strategists, Information Architects, Interaction Designers, User Researchers, Content Strategists, Visual and Data Visualization Designers, and Developers -- And never forgot to have a ton of fun along the way.

I've designed highly transactional programs and digital experiences for clients such as Boston Consulting Group, Allstate, Discover Financial Services, Cox Communications, Wal-Mart, Sam's Club, The Coca Cola Company, Pizza Hut, Norwegian Cruise Lines, as well as engagements with industry leading pharmaceutical and consumer packaged goods companies such as Eli Lilly and Company and Proctor & Gamble.

My interest and ongoing research includes omni-channel digital engagement that lives at the intersection of product design, entertainment and utility.

I hold degrees in Architecture and Computer Information Systems.

I look forward to connecting!
