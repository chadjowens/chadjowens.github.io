The Assignment

While working with Discover Financial Services, I had the opportunity to work with the mobile team and lead several projects to improve the mobile and tablet product experiences. The first project I worked on was for a complete user interface overhaul and to introduce the Shop Discover rewards program to mobile and tablet. Part of this effort included a major audit to make sure desktop, tablet and handset experiences were starting to utilize the same design language.

Sample rapid design lab sketches

We began each engagement by inviting key business stakeholders to our offices for a half-day working session.  During these sessions, we reviewed key areas of stimulus such as Heuristics and light Competitive Analysis.

Additionally, through our Rapid Design Lab methodology, we reviewed sketches of the many ways the product experience could work. The intent of this exercise was to uncover areas of product and experience-based differentiation.

App maps and functional workflows

User testing with Axure and annotated wireframes

As we honed in on possible solutions, we developed prototypes of navigation concepts and key features of the experience to test early with users. After early conceptual user testing had given us directional inputs, in most cases, we developed detailed wireframes.
‍

Uniform vs. unified design: driving cross-channel consistency

During this particular engagement, our focus was not on just tablet and mobile design. We developed an audit system to evaluate content, labels, and interactive elements across desktop, mobile an tablet design to start to influence a more consistent experience.

Sample iPad screens with visual design applied

Sample iPhone screens with annotations

iPhone handset design needed to be adjusted in many cases due to the obvious ergonomic and form factor differences.

Final legal review and technology hand-off documentation delivered was a combination of designed screens (showing all states and behaviors) with detailed interaction notes.

Business Benefits

  1. Drive commerce opportunities with introduction of tablet-optimized commerce functionality (ShopDiscover) and Discover Offers portal
  2. Lift in rewards activity by increasing Cashback Bonus (CBB) awareness through improved push alerts and merchant map/locator
  3. Increase payment velocity with pay- by-push support
  4. Improve customer service experience through in-app features such as viewing and saving statement PDFs and Lost/Stolen reporting.

Process & Activities Overview 

  1. Rapid Design Lab working sessions to uncover design innovation opportunities and validate implementation complexity within agile sprint windows
  2. Detailed functional requirements gathering and project definition
  3. Full interaction design concepts and Axure prototype for concept testing and design research
  4. Complete interaction design documentation and interaction specifications
  5. Visual design
  6. Iterative design recommendations based on User Research findings
