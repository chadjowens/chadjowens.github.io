Pizza Hut

Ordering Experience Redesign

Overview

Pizza Hut engaged IMC2 to help them rethink the online ordering experience. This was firs major redesign of that experience in many years. Throughout this assignment, our design team had to dive very deep into the technology capabilities  and work with Pizza Hut's 3rd party online ordering solution provider to understand capabilities and constraints around introducing new ways of moving a consumer through the ordering path.

We introduced many new features such as allowing a consumer to save favorites or reordering from order history to help remove friction and increase ordering time. Many of those features continue to hold up today. Once live, this ordering experience remained unchanged for many years.
‍

Visual Design

Visual design focused on reinforcing a very simple experience even through a multistep process with many decision points.
‍

Detailed Interaction Design

The site redesign focused on removing a lot of information on the sit and focusing the user's attention on the menu and ordering flow. The site experience was essentially refactored to be a funnel to ordering.

Once bigger navigational and site structure items out of the way, a lot of focus and energy was put on "merchandising and cross-sell / up-sell" capabilities.

Additionally, another area of exploration was how can a user streamline their future experience based on their current order effort.

This touched on notions of "Investment" and "Rewards" even though this project predated more recent ideas from the Hook Model and other Persuasion Design concepts.

‍

System Workflow Diagrams

Detailed workflow diagrams were created so that the design team, client and 3rd party technology provider could be on the same page when discussing how the current state technology platform worked and to make sure we were all aligned on feasibility around new design recommendations
