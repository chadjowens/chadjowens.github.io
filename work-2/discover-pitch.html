Discover Financial Services

New Business Pitch Experience Concepts

The Assignment

When I first joined FCB Chicago, I was immediately pulled into a large new business pitch effort for Discover Financial Services. I oversaw the effort from an Experience Design perspective and ran the creation of a personas, a newly created information architecture scheme, interaction design and visual design recommendations. After winning the pitch,  I was focused on the Discover Financial Services business for the next couple of years while working at FCB.
‍

Sample Screen Designs

Visual design followed the interaction design wireframe screens. For the pitch, I focused on certain sections of the site such as simplifying the account creation process, better integrating the Shop Discover rewards program experience and simplifying the account statement experience.
‍

Sample Interaction Design with Detailed Annotations

Detailed interaction designs were created that tied to core behavioral economics (and persuasion design) principals that were outlined by the brand strategy team as key opportunities to differentiate the brand experience.
‍

Sample Site Architecture Showing User Flows

Part of the pitch effort included recommendations of how to improve the overall site architecture. For these recommendations, I developed a new site map and navigation schema. I also used this to help orient the audience on how the different personas would experience the site and based on their specific needs how they would likely navigate to reach their specific "Key Moments."
