Jack Daniels "Sala Siete"

Content strategy for national latin music sweepstakes and promotion

The Assignment

Jack Daniels partnered with FCB to develop promotion and activation plan for an upcoming concert series that Jack Daniels had been sponsoring for several years. The assignment for the promotion was to develop concepts and ideas for how to make the experience "even bigger and more engaging."

The resulting concept and recommendation was a very large and multi-faceted sweepstakes and contest that unfolded over the course of several months leading up to large well known music festival. The overall experience spanned local and national music events and unfolded both across physical and digital experiences.

Defining the audience and "techographic"

  • As this promotional is specifically geared to Latin American audience, defining the target audience was the first step in the assignment.
  • We not only defined behavioral attributes for this particular audience persona but also a "technographic" view of how this persona interacts with and consumes digital content.

Defining the mechanics of the promotion
  • Defining the facets of the promotion was key due to the sheer scale.
  • This promotion had two key aspects. the first was a general sweepstakes entry that would allow a winner a chance to travel to a large prominent multi-day music festival.
  • The second aspect of the promotion is a contest that allows the winner to serve as the social media reporter for the entire event with exclusive back stage access that only Jack Daniels could provide.

The "Digital Hub" concept
  • A large part of the experience includes a mobile optimized digital hub that provides a range of content to build awareness and engagement for participants throughout the concert series leading up to the main music festival event.

Content prioritization and touchpoints
  • A content calendar was then created to show what type of content would be delivered, during which phase of the contest and through which media channel.
  • This plan also highlights which features will be rolled out for the digital hub as the program evolves over time.
  • A roadmap defining the purpose for key social media channels was created. This included a recommendation for how content should be delivered through each of the various social media touchpoints.
